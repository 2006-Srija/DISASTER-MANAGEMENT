Medical Assistance During Disaster

During a disaster, it's essential to know where and how to seek medical assistance. Here are a few key steps you can follow:

Steps to Get Medical Help

  • Contact your nearest medical facility for immediate help.
  • Dial emergency services (Ambulance: 102 in India).
  • Identify nearby hospitals equipped to handle disaster-related injuries.
  • Follow guidelines provided by local authorities.

Emergency Contacts

Here are some emergency numbers you may need:

  • Emergency Ambulance: 102
  • Police: 100
  • Fire: 101
